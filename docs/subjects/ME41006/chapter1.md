## Intended Learning Outcomes

Students will able to understand the basic mechanisms of heat transfer, which are:
- Conduction
- Convection
- Radiation 
- Fourier's law of heat conduction
- Newton's law of cooling
- Stefan–Boltzmann law of radiation
